# SETU Groups — final logo decision

## Selected master logo
Use the uploaded intertwined bridge mark as the primary SETU Groups corporate logo.

## Why this is the best fit
This mark supports the three meanings that matter most for SETU:

1. **Data flow**
   The curved, crossing lines suggest movement of information through connected systems.

2. **Supply chain routes**
   The directional sweeps read naturally as trade paths, logistics motion, and routed operations.

3. **Interconnected systems**
   The intertwined structure communicates orchestration rather than isolated tools.

4. **Brand meaning of SETU**
   The center span reads as a bridge, aligning directly to the meaning of the name.

## Recommended usage system
- `logo.png` → corporate website header, proposals, company pages
- `symbol.png` → product icon base, favicon source, app shell branding

## Future refinement direction
The next upgrade should be a clean SVG redraw of the symbol with:
